Xiaobin Liu

I received the B.E. degree from Nankai University in 2016, and the Ph.D. degree from Peking University in Jan. 2022. My research interests include image retrieval, object re-identification, and deep metric learning. I joined PCG, Tencent in 2022.02, my work focuses on content processing and analysis.
