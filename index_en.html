Xiaobin Liu

I received the B.E. degree from Nankai University in 2016, and the Ph.D. degree from Peking University in Jan. 2022. I was a senior researcher with focus on multimodal content understanding in PCG, Tencent from 2022.02 to 2024.08. I am now a assistant (postdoctoral) researcher in Laboratory for Advanced Perception and Control (LAPC), College of AI, Nankai University. My work focuses on embodied AI and multimodal content understanding. I have authored/co-authored 12 papers on import journals/conferences such as TIP, PR, IJCAI, ACM MM. I lead 1 NSFC grant, 1 Postdoctoral Fellowship Program of CPSF, and paticipate multiple NSFC/National Key R&D Program grants. I obtained the National Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award in Transportation Engineering. I serve as a reviewer for more than 10 journals/conferes, including TIP, IJCV, CVPR, ICCV, etc.
